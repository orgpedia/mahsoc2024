The Proposal: -
A total of 353 government residential primary schools, one in each taluk, for boys and girls belonging to Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes, have been approved to be set up in the state by the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance. Accordingly, in the first phase, Yoo WRATH Primary Residential Schools have been approved and a total of 92 Government Residential Schools have been started till date. The number of students allowed in these government residential schools is as high as 18400. Government Residential Schools are doing a commendable job in increasing the enrolment of students in Government Residential Schools and ensuring their social and educational progress. The students admitted to Government Residential Schools under the Department are provided with facilities like free accommodation, food arrangements, health facilities, sports facilities, educational materials, boarding and lodging, living allowance, etc. as per the provision prescribed under the Department of Social Justice and Special Assistance, Government Decision, dated 26th July, 2011. Pursuant to the proposal submitted by the Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune under reference No. 4, the Government was considering the purchase of school uniforms and PT uniforms for the students of Government Residential Schools for the academic year 2026-27.
Government Decision: - The estimated amount for purchase of School Uniform (per 2 nos) b PT Uniform (per 1 nos) for the students enrolled in Government Residential Schools for SC / ST newly educated boys and girls under Social Justice and Special Assistance Department is Rs. 7,25,40, 216 / - (Rs. Procurement amounting to ₹70 million TAA (Rupees Forty Two Thousand and Sixteen Only) is being approved by the Administration through e-tendering process subject to the following conditions as provided in the Government Decision dated 2nd December, 2016 of the Department of Industries, Power and Labour.
a) As per and in accordance with the provisions prescribed under the Government Decision of the Department of Industries, Power and Labour, dated 1st December, 2016, to procure School Uniforms (per 2 nos) and PT Uniforms (per 1 nos) under the Facilitation Scheme for Government Residential School Admitted Students for Scheduled Castes and Newly Abled Boys and Girls, from time to time.
